# Verdant Aqua — Palette Reference

A nature-inspired gradient that runs from near-black forest floor through
rich greens and emeralds into luminous aquamarine and a crystalline white peak.

## Gradient stops

```
#AAFFF0  ████  8  — crystalline near-white aqua   (absolute peaks)
#50FFF0  ████  7  — electric seafoam               (air / transients)
#00F6D8  ████  6  — bright cyan-aqua               (presence)
#00E1C0  ████  5  — pure aquamarine                (high-mids)
#2ED8A2  ████  4  — vivid emerald                  (midrange)
#1A9C6B  ████  3  — rich forest-to-teal            (bass)
#0D4025  ████  2  — deep undergrowth               (sub-bass)
#071109  ████  1  — near-black forest floor        (bar roots)
```

Background: `#0B1A14`

## Mood

Professional, organic, alive. Dark enough to feel serious, vibrant enough
to catch eyes across a room. The aqua peaks glow against the forest-dark
background like bioluminescence.

## Pairing tips

Terminal background: `#0B1A14` (match exactly — the bars blend into the bg seamlessly)
Font: Iosevka / Maple Mono / any soft-geometry Nerd Font
Font size: 8–10pt (more rows → more gradient → more drama)
Window: borderless / transparent title bar if your terminal supports it
